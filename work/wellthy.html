Wellthy

  • Front-end development
  • June 2015
  • www.wellthy.com

While on co-op at Ronik I worked on a team that created a web application for Wellthy, a health startup. Wellthy aims to provide affordable services to people who are caring for their sick or injured loved ones. They employ care coordinators who are tasked with helping specific customers. I worked on part of the web application that you have to be logged in to see. The client has control over some colors and styles, so the site may not appear as it did when we first launched it.

When we first began working on this project we created a static version of the site while we waited for Wellthy's developers to finish the back-end. The static version of the site was created with Slush Stencil, so we templated in Swig and styled in Stylus. Once the back-end was complete, we converted our static pages to the Django site.
