Transforming Medicine

  • Front-end development
  • February 2015
  • www.transformingmedicine.org

While on co-op at Ronik I worked on a team that built a marketing site for Transforming Medicine. Transforming Medicine is a non-profit organization that is aiming to match people with life-threatening illnesses to the best treatments for their condition. The client has control over the background image and the content, so the live site may not appear exactly as it did when we first launched it.

We set up a CMS on Webhook, used Slush Stencil to scaffold the site, templated in Swig, and styled in Stylus.
