Human Ventures

  • Front-end development
  • March 2015

While on co-op at Ronik I worked on a team that built a responsive landing page for Human Ventures that could excite investors. The text on the landing page was unveiled via elegant scroll animations. Human Ventures is a startup studio that is committed to helping early-stage startups with talent, leadership, and resources. I worked on the front-end of the site with Max Lever, who was another co-op. In December 2015, Human Ventures changed their website in an effort to showcase their new portfolio work, so our work is no longer live on their site.

We set up a CMS on Webhook, used Slush Stencil to scaffold the site, and styled in Stylus. For the numerous scroll interactions we used ScrollMagic.

Scroll Animations

The landing page had many text animations that occurred on scroll. These were done with ScrollMagic, as previously stated. We set up waypoints that would trigger certain animations, and calculated various CSS properties based on the progress of the scroll.
